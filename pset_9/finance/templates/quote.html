Kelly Tung!

Home Pets Sleeping Foodie

I'm a HUGE foodie... my favorite food is matcha! To celebrate my love for matcha, I'm going to rank the best and worst matcha lattes I've tried in Harvard Square!

Cafe Rating Why I rated it like this
Pavement ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ Literally so freaking amazing. Perfect sugar amount, tastes like genuine matcha, is the perfect sizing. It's also so convenient for me because I live in Matthews so I can always get it within 5 minutes. The only downside is the price, but I won't mark it down for that.
Kung Fu Tea ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ Solid choice. Has a good, yummy taste and you can set the sugar content to what you want. Ordering here makes me feel guilty, though, because it's more of a "sweet" than "coffee." But it's well-made and worth the price!
Cafe Gato Rojo ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ Okay, so I admit that I rated this a little more than it should've gotten. But it really does keep me awake and the best part is that it's FREE because I always use BoardPlus when ordering here. That alone gives it an additional star. The matcha itself is just okay, not great but also not bad. I always finish it within a few minutes, haha.
Blue Bottle Coffee ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆ I'm actually sad about giving this one such a low rating, but it had to be done. The taste itself is actually quite authentic, but a little too milky/diluted for my taste. Also, they expect you to add your own sweetener, but it just doesn't do it for me. It ends up being bland, and whatever sugar I add in tastes artificial. Plus, it's a bit of a walk to get there and it's also not cheap either.
