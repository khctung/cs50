Home Life at Harvard Pets Sleeping Foodie

I'm a HUGE foodie... my favorite food is matcha! To celebrate my love for matcha, I'm going to rank the best and worst matcha lattes I've tried in Harvard Square!

Cafe Rating Why I rated it like this
Pavement ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ Literally so freaking amazing. Perfect sugar amount, tastes like genuine matcha, is the perfect sizing. It's also so convenient for me because I live in Matthews so I can always get it within 5 minutes. The only downside is the price, but I won't mark it down for that.
Java Junction ★ ★ ★ ⯨ ☆ Good coffee, but limited seating.
Starbucks ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆ I was torn between 3 and 4 stars, but ultimately decided to go with 3. To start, it is WAY too sweet. Unfortunately, the sugar is part of Starbucks' matcha powder, so if I wanted less sugar, I'd also get less matcha. I will deduct a star for the price because it's just not good enough to justify the $5+ I spent on a SMALL cup of coffee. The flavor is decent, though.
